About Us

Established to take a different approach.

At ESPA, we believe infrastructure, land development, and construction projects achieve the best outcomes when they are well coordinated from the outset, with a clear focus on the end result.

When consultants work within their own scope, it can limit alignment across the wider project and impact efficiency through design and approvals.

By being involved early, the focus is on providing clear, practical advice aligned with the end goal from day one. Through the planning and design phases, projects are actively coordinated across the design team, ensuring the right people are involved and working together.

ESPA provides Civil Engineering, Surveying, Project Management, and Advisory services to support this approach, bringing together the key elements of a project to deliver joined up, practical outcomes.

The result is a smoother path through approvals, more efficient design outcomes, and greater confidence at every stage of the project.

What We Do

Decades of Experience.
One Clear Focus.

ESPA is driven by the client’s ultimate outcome, ensuring every service we provide actively supports the wider design team in delivering it.

Engineering

Our civil engineering services focus on the design and coordination of earthworks, roading, drainage, and services to support efficient, buildable, and compliant projects.

From initial feasibility through to detailed design, the emphasis is on developing practical solutions that respond to site constraints and align with project objectives.

This includes earthworks design, roading layouts, stormwater and wastewater systems, potable water supply, and the integration of underground services such as power and telecommunications.

Close coordination with consultants ensures designs are aligned across the project, reducing risk during approvals and construction.

The outcome is clear, coordinated civil design that is efficient to construct, meets regulatory requirements, and supports a smooth path through approvals and delivery.

Surveying

ESPA provides Engineering and Cadastral (Legal) Surveying services to support projects from early planning through to construction and completion.

Our surveying services include:

  • Topographical Surveys
  • Site Surveys
  • Set Out Surveys
  • Monitoring Surveys, including precise levelling
  • Siting and HIRB Surveys
  • Land Transfer Surveys
  • Legalisation Surveys
  • Unit Title Surveys
  • Cross Lease Conversions and Updates
  • Boundary Redefinition Surveys
  • Removal of Limitation Surveys

Our broad range of surveying services ensures the right level of information is captured and delivered at each stage of a project. The focus is on accuracy, clarity, and providing data that integrates seamlessly with the wider design and delivery process.

Project Management

ESPA identified a gap in how projects are managed through the planning, resource consent, and design stages. Too often, consultants focus on their individual scope, resulting in outputs that are technically sound, but not aligned with the client’s overall objectives.

ESPA takes responsibility for coordinating the project during these critical stages, providing clients with a single, informed point of contact. Our focus remains on the end outcome, ensuring decisions made along the way support a compliant, buildable result aligned with the client’s goals.

This is achieved by bringing the design team together and managing it as one, ensuring a coordinated approach. This includes managing programme and deliverables, leading the resource consent process, reviewing and challenging draft conditions, and aligning the team through detailed design to ensure the design is suitable for building consent and practical to construct.

As projects move toward construction, ESPA continues to support our clients through the tender process, ensuring design intent is maintained and that information provided to contractors is clear, coordinated, and complete.

Advisory

ESPA provides early stage advice that empowers clients to make informed decisions, with a clear understanding of the wider design, approval, and delivery process.

With a strong understanding of the full project lifecycle, from feasibility through to completion, ESPA offers practical, honest advice focused on achieving the best outcome. Central to this is keeping the client’s objectives front of mind, ensuring early decisions align with what the project is ultimately trying to achieve. This may involve identifying risks early, guiding where additional input is needed, or advising on the viability of a project or specific approach.

ESPA has experience working with a wide range of stakeholders, including local and central government, NZTA, KiwiRail, Iwi, environmental groups, and landowners. This enables clear navigation of stakeholder expectations and supports productive engagement throughout the project.

Drawing on extensive local and international experience, our advice is grounded in a forward-thinking approach, focusing on what is possible, identifying opportunities, and finding practical ways to achieve successful outcomes.

By engaging ESPA early in an advisory role, clients benefit from:

  • Clear, informed decision making from the outset
  • Early identification of risks and constraints
  • Better alignment between project objectives and design outcomes
  • Improved navigation of planning and stakeholder requirements
  • Greater confidence in the feasibility and direction of the project
